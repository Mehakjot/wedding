Catering



We offer on-premise, off-premise catering as well as different types of cuisines. Some of the most popular cuisines in demand are Punjabi, South Indian, Rajasthani, Chinese, Italian, Hyderabadi as well as Continental, Mexican, and Lebanese. Whether you want pure veg or non-veg mix, we can cater to all your needs. Try our free tasting service before you make up your mind and decide to hire our services.


Wedding Inivitations



Even in this technologically advanced day and age, traditional wedding invitations are the best way to invite guests to your wedding. That’s where we at Turmeric Ink, step in. Our range of invitation cards can also be used for events like Anniversaries, Birthdays, Baby Showers, etc. We also deal in stationery and wedding collaterals. From cotton, linen paper cards to imported high end paper and gift bags, we have it all covered for you!
